SIONT UK LTD

Professional Data Center Services based in UK

SIONT UK LTD is a private limited company headquartered in the United Kingdom, operating within the information technology and telecommunications sectors. Our team consists of highly experienced professionals specializing in setting up, managing, and maintaining world-class data centers.

We offer end-to-end data center solutions — from provisioning and physical setup to ongoing maintenance and responsible decommissioning. Our services are tailored to fit the needs of organizations of all sizes.

24/7 Support Certified Technicians Customized Solutions Enterprise Experience

Services

Provisioning

Strategic planning and implementation of data center infrastructure, including hardware selection, layout design, and capacity planning.

Rack & Stack

Professional installation of servers, network devices, and storage systems with proper spacing, weight distribution, and airflow optimization.

Cabling

Structured cabling installation with precise management, proper labeling, and optimized routing to support airflow and simplify maintenance.

Maintenance

Preventative maintenance and troubleshooting to ensure optimal hardware performance, reduce downtime, and extend equipment lifespan.

Monitoring

24/7 monitoring with real-time alerts, performance tracking, and detailed reporting to ensure reliability and early detection of issues.

Patching & Decommissioning

Systematic firmware/software updates and secure hardware decommissioning with certified data destruction and eco-friendly disposal.
